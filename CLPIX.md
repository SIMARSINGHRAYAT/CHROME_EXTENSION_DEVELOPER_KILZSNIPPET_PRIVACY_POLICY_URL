# Privacy Policy for CLIPIX

**Effective Date:** April 24, 2026

## 1. Introduction
CLIPIX is a lightweight application designed to allow users to paste and download screenshots directly from their clipboard. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how the application operates.

## 2. Information We Collect
CLIPIX does **not collect, store, or transmit any personal data**.

- No user accounts are required
- No personal identifiers are collected
- No analytics or tracking mechanisms are used
- No data is sent to external servers

## 3. Clipboard Data Handling
CLIPIX accesses clipboard data **only when the user explicitly pastes (Ctrl + V)** within the application interface.

- The clipboard content (image) is processed **locally in the user's browser**
- The data is **never uploaded, stored, or shared**
- The image remains entirely under user control

## 4. Data Storage
CLIPIX does not use any form of backend storage, database, or cloud service.

- All operations occur locally on the user's device
- Downloaded files are saved directly by the user

## 5. Third-Party Services
CLIPIX does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or external platforms.

## 6. Security
Since CLIPIX does not collect or transmit data, there is minimal risk of data exposure. All processing occurs locally within the user's environment.

## 7. Children's Privacy
CLIPIX does not knowingly collect any data from children or users of any age.

## 8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy in the future. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this document.

## 9. Contact Information
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:

**Email:** support@clipix.app

## Summary (Plain English)
CLIPIX works entirely on your device. Your screenshots never leave your computer, and we do not collect or track any of your data.
